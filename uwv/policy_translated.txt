Send an email to cvd@uwv.nl as soon as possible. Include the following:

- The IP address or URL in question.
- A description of the problem.
- How we can reproduce and investigate the problem.
- Your name, phone number, and email address.

Encrypt the email with our PGP key.

After your report
You will receive an acknowledgment of receipt within 1 day. We will respond to your report within 5 days. We will also keep you informed thereafter.

Never share the information with others. Take responsibility and do not go further than necessary to demonstrate the problem. This means:

- Do not install malware.
- Do not copy, change, or delete data. An alternative is a directory listing of the system.

- Do not make any system changes.

- Do not break into the system multiple times.
- Do not use brute force to gain access.
- Do not use denial-of-service or social engineering. No legal consequences

If you adhere to these rules, we will not file a complaint against you. We treat your report with strict confidentiality. We also do not share personal data with others without your permission, unless legally required or compelled by a court ruling.

If you make a significant contribution to the security of our digital services, we may include your name in our Hall of Fame (English). We do not offer a monetary reward.
